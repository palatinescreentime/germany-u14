📱 Getting Started

Add the app to your iPhone home screen so it opens full-screen with no browser bar:

1Open germany.bargraph.city in Safari
2Tap the Share button (box with arrow) at the bottom
3Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
4Tap Add — the G icon appears on your home screen
Parent view
Share germany.bargraph.city/?view=parent with parents. They see a tile for every game — tap any game for the live score, countdown clock, formation with player names, and match events. Use the QR button on the Live tab to share on the sideline instantly.
📋 Before Game Day
Roster & Team Name
  • Tap Roster from the season screen
  • Tap any player row to edit name, grade, foot, positions, or goalie willingness
  • Tap + Add Player to add  ·  tap × to remove
  • Team name is editable at the top of the Roster page
Setting Up a Game
  • Tap any game card from the season screen
  • Setup tab — enter opponent, date, location (auto-saves on exit)
  • Toggle availability — tap a name to mark OUT
  • Coach Notes — type tactical notes and tap Save Notes
  • Status updates automatically: Upcoming → Live when clock starts → Complete when final score submitted
Building the Lineup
  • Tap Lineup tab → choose First Half or Second Half — each half has its own independent lineup
  • Pick a formation: 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 4-5-1, 3-5-2
  • Tap a player in the Player Pool at the bottom, then tap a position slot on the pitch to place them
  • Tap any placed player to select, tap another to swap
  • Tap the arrow label below each bench player to set their planned sub target — carries through to the Live pitch as a gold outline
  • Copy from game imports any previous lineup including bench and sub targets
  • Tap Print — prints larger font with position tags and planned sub targets shown
H2 rule
All six H1 bench players should start H2. Gold-bordered chips show who is new to the pitch that half.
⏱ Game Day — Live Tab

The pitch appears first on mobile. Scroll down slightly to reach the clock controls.

Reading the Pitch
  • Players shown in actual current positions, updating after every sub
  • Gold outline on a red chip = planned sub target — that player is coming off soon
  • Blue outline on a red chip = selected for a position swap with another field player
  • No outline = no sub planned for that player
  • Dark red + ⚠ = marked unavailable in Setup but on the pitch
Clock
▶Start at kickoff — sets status to Live automatically
⏸Pause (halftime, stoppage)
±Adjust — type the actual minute if you forgot to start on time
↺Reset to 35:00 for the new half
QRShow QR code — parents scan to open the parent view

Counts down from 35:00. Gold color = overtime. Parents see the same clock on their view.

Making Substitutions — bench players
  • Tap 1 — tap a bench player chip. It turns gold. The header updates to guide you.
  • Tap 2 — tap any player on the pitch. They become the sub target (gold arrow label updates). Bench player stays selected.
  • Tap 3 — tap the bench player again. Confirm bar appears: "Subbing Wyatt for Ryu · CM"
  • ✓ Confirm — logs the sub, updates the pitch instantly
  • Adjust Sub — keeps bench player selected so you can tap a different field player to change the target
  • Cancel — deselects and returns to normal
Pre-planned
If you set sub targets on the Lineup page, the bench player already has a target when you hit Tap 1 — just tap them twice total to confirm.
Swapping field players
  • Tap 1 — tap any on-field player. Their chip turns blue.
  • Tap 2 — tap a second on-field player. Confirm bar appears on the pitch: "Move Jonathan (ST) ↔ Ryu (CM)?"
  • Tap ✓ Confirm to swap their positions · tap Cancel to clear
  • Tap the selected player again to deselect without swapping
Tracking the Score
  • Tap Goal — shows all roster players split into on-field and bench. Germany score auto-increments.
  • For opponent goals: tap the + button under the opponent score — instant, no typing
  • Tap − to correct an accidental opponent goal entry
  • Both scores update on the parent view immediately
Ending the Game
  • Scroll to the Score card on the Live tab
  • Confirm both scores are correct, then tap Submit Final Score & Unlock Report
  • Status changes to Complete · Report tab unlocks · game tile shows green dot on season screen
🧱 Awarding Bag Tags During / After a Game

The 🧱 Tags tab lives between Live and Report on every game. Use it to award tags without leaving the game view.

Tag colors in the game Tags tab

RyuAwarded this game

Ryu ×3Awarded this game AND received before (count = season total)
  • Tap any chip to award or remove — no limits, all tags can be awarded multiple times to any player
  • Badge count = season total across all games

👥 Parent View
  • URL: germany.bargraph.city/?view=parent
  • Game tile grid — tap any game to see formation, score, clock, and events
  • Pitch updates as subs are made · score updates instantly on goal or + tap
  • Auto-refreshes every 20 seconds
📊 Bag Tags & Stats
  • Bag Tags → Award Tags — select game, tap player chips. Season total shown as ×N.
  • Season Grid — full player × tag table with counts
  • Season Stats — unlocks after first completed game. Cumulative minutes, goals, assists.

🗑 Clearing Game Data

Setup tab → Danger Zone → Clear Game Data. Resets lineup, events, score, status, and clock. Keeps opponent name, date, location, and notes.
